About Luzen
2003

We design environments that transcend the ordinary — where light, material, and form converge into spaces that feel both inevitable and deeply personal.

Founded in Oslo in 2003, Luzen Studio has spent two decades shaping homes, cultural institutions, and commercial spaces across Europe. Our approach begins with listening — then designing with intention.
