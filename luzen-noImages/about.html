Our Story

Architecture & interior design from Oslo — shaping light, material and form since 2003.

Founded 2003

Two Decades of Shaping Space

Luzen Studio was founded in Oslo in 2003 by architects Maren Solvang and Erik Holt — two practitioners who shared a conviction that architecture could be both rigorous and warm, ambitious and considerate of the human scale.

Over two decades, we have grown from a two-person practice into a studio of thirty-two designers, project managers, and craftspeople spread across Oslo, Vienna, and Porto. Our projects span continents but our methodology remains constant: listen deeply, observe carefully, and design with intention.

We believe that the best architecture emerges from an honest dialogue between client, context, and culture — and that the measure of a great building is how it feels to inhabit it on a Tuesday morning, not just how it photographs from the outside.

Our Philosophy

What We Believe In

Every decision we make — from the orientation of a window to the selection of a door handle — is guided by a set of convictions about what architecture should be and do.

01

Context is Everything

A building is not an object — it is a response. Every project begins with an intensive reading of the site, its history, its microclimate, and its social fabric.

02

Restraint as Craft

We resist the temptation to add. The discipline of editing — knowing what to leave out — is where the real skill of design lives.

03

Material Honesty

We use materials in ways that respect their nature. Concrete is massive and permanent. Timber is warm and alive. We don't disguise one thing as another.

04

Long-term Thinking

Buildings outlast their architects. Every project is designed to grow more beautiful with age, to serve the third and fourth generation of occupants as well as the first.

How We Work

Our Design Process

01

Discovery

We begin with an extended conversation — listening to your aspirations, constraints, and the story you want your space to tell. We visit the site multiple times, at different hours, in different light.

02

Concept

From our research, we distil a single organising idea — a concept that answers the brief and gives the design its internal logic. Everything that follows refers back to this.

03

Development

The concept is tested, refined, and developed into detailed drawings, material samples, and physical or digital models. We present options and iterate collaboratively until the design is right.

04

Delivery

We manage the construction process with the same care as the design — on-site, closely engaged, solving problems in real time and ensuring the vision is realised to the last detail.
