What We Offer

Architecture, interior design, and landscape — conceived as one discipline, delivered as one team.

Our Disciplines

Full-Spectrum Design Services

Whether you're commissioning a new building, transforming an existing space, or seeking expert guidance through the planning process, Luzen Studio offers a complete range of architectural and design services delivered to the highest standard.

We work across scales — from a single apartment to a cultural campus — and across sectors: residential, commercial, hospitality, and public. Every project, regardless of size, receives the same rigorous attention and creative investment.

01

Architectural Design

Our core discipline — creating buildings from the inside out, from the first sketch to the final snag. We handle every stage of the architectural process: feasibility assessment, concept design, planning permission, technical documentation, and construction oversight.

We specialise in residential architecture (private houses, multi-family housing, renovations) as well as commercial, cultural, and hospitality projects. Our buildings are known for their spatial intelligence, material sensitivity, and enduring quality.

Services include: site analysis, concept design, planning application, design development, construction documentation, tender and contract administration, site inspection.

02

Interior Design

Interior design at Luzen is inseparable from architecture — we do not decorate spaces, we complete them. Our interior design service addresses the full spatial experience: room proportions, ceiling heights, light quality, material palette, furniture arrangement, and the selection of every surface and fixture.

We work with both new builds (where interior design can be fully integrated from the start) and existing spaces undergoing transformation. Our material library spans natural stone, artisanal tiles, hand-woven textiles, custom joinery, and contemporary furniture from leading European makers.

03

Spatial Planning

Spatial planning is the art of making a given footprint work as hard as possible — choreographing movement, allocating programme, and creating a hierarchy of spaces that feels logical and generous at the same time.

We offer standalone spatial planning as an early-stage service: before a client commits to a full architectural brief, we can help them understand what a site or building could accommodate, and how different configurations would perform in use. This service is particularly valuable for developers, investors, and institutions considering new acquisitions or developments.

04

Renovation & Restoration

Some of our most rewarding projects involve buildings that already exist — structures with history, character, and accumulated meaning that deserve to be preserved and renewed rather than replaced.

Our renovation and restoration service combines a forensic understanding of existing buildings with a rigorous design sensibility for what they should become. We are experienced in working within listed and heritage-protected buildings, coordinating with conservation officers and specialist contractors to ensure that changes are appropriate, technically sound, and beautifully executed.

05

Landscape Architecture

The best architecture does not end at the building line. Our landscape architecture service designs the outdoor environment as an extension of the building — calibrated to the same spatial principles, material palette, and long-term ambitions.

Led by Jonas Veth, our landscape team works on private gardens, courtyard spaces, public realm, and complex multi-building sites. We design with native and adapted plant species, sustainable drainage systems, and an understanding of how spaces change across the seasons and years.

06

Design Consulting

Not every client needs a full architectural service. Some need an experienced eye and an independent perspective at a critical moment — a second opinion on a design, expert input into a brief, or strategic advice on how to approach a complex development.

Our consulting service provides access to Luzen's design expertise on a flexible, project-by-project basis. It is particularly suited to developers, large organisations with in-house teams, and clients who need specialist knowledge to navigate a specific challenge.
